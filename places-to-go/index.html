Where to go?

Gastown
Vancouver’s oldest neighbourhood, Gastown, has kept its historic charm and independent spirit since 1886. Take a picture at Maple Tree Square!
Stanley Park
A magnificent green oasis in the midst of the urban landscape, Stanley Park.
Explore this 1000-acre public grounds or enjoy the scenic view by bicycle!
Capilano River
The Capilano River Suspension Bridge offers Canyon Lights, Guided Tours and Adventures through the Living Forest.
Are you brave enough to cross?
Kitsilano
A lively, beautiful, and eco-friendly beachside community forms the heart of Kitsilano. Grab a drink and chill out with the locals at Kits beach.
Granville Island
Vancouver’s premier artistic & cultural hub, Granville Island. Explore the marketplace, discover unique paintings and grab yourself a souvenir!
Deep Cove
Deep Cove is one of many scenic spots that North Vancouver has to offer.
Grab your hiking boots and check out the stunning view at Quarry Rock!
